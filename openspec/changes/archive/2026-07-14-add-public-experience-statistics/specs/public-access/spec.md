## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Public statistics presentation

The system SHALL show visitors aggregate public-experience statistics on the
statistics page, including all-time and preceding-90-day totals,
recommendations, and real-life meetings.

#### Scenario: Visitor views the statistics page

- **WHEN** a visitor opens the statistics page
- **THEN** the page displays the aggregate public-experience totals, recommendation figures that exclude skipped answers from their percentage, and real-life-meeting figures
- **AND** the page does not require authentication
